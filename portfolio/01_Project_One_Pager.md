# LaunchIQ — Project One-Pager

**AI-Powered Product Launch Intelligence Platform**
*Built by Venkata Anil Kumar — AI Engineer*

---

## The Problem

Marketing teams and founders spend 2–3 weeks manually researching competitors, defining audiences, and writing launch strategies — only to miss the optimal launch window or misalign messaging with market reality. There is no intelligent, unified system that automates launch intelligence end-to-end.

---

## The Solution

**LaunchIQ** is a multi-agent AI SaaS platform that autonomously researches your market, generates your go-to-market strategy, creates launch content, and tracks execution — delivering a complete launch playbook in under 10 minutes.

```
Product Description → 6 AI Agents → Complete Launch Strategy + Content
```

---

## How It Works

| Step | Agent | Output |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1 | Orchestrator (Opus 4.6) | Workflow plan, agent coordination |
| 2 | Market Intelligence (Sonnet 4.6) | Competitor brief + trend signals |
| 3 | Audience Insight (Sonnet 4.6) | 3 buyer personas + messaging angles |
| 4 | Launch Strategy (Opus 4.6) | Phased GTM plan with milestones |
| 5 | Content Generation (Sonnet 4.6) | Email, social, and ad copy |
| 6 | Analytics & Feedback (Haiku 4.5) | Performance tracking + optimization |

Human-in-the-loop checkpoints at every stage — AI does the work, human approves.
