## 1. Product Vision & Mission

**Vision:**
> A world where any founder or marketer can launch any product with the intelligence of a seasoned marketing team — regardless of their team size or budget.

**Mission:**
> LaunchIQ uses autonomous AI agents to democratize product launch excellence — making world-class market research, strategy, and content creation accessible to every product builder.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Launching a product is one of the most complex, high-stakes tasks in business. Yet the process remains largely manual, fragmented, and inaccessible to those without large marketing teams or budgets.

**The current reality:**
- Market research takes 2–3 weeks of manual effort
- Competitive analysis is done in spreadsheets that go stale immediately
- Launch strategies live in disconnected slide decks with no execution link
- Content creation is slow, repetitive, and disconnected from market context
- Post-launch learning rarely happens — there is no feedback loop

**The result:** Slower launches, misaligned messaging, missed windows, and wasted budget.

## 4. Product Overview

LaunchIQ is a **multi-agent AI SaaS platform** that automates the full product launch intelligence workflow.

**How it works:**
1. User inputs their product description (plain English, ~200 words)
2. Orchestrator Agent activates and dispatches 5 specialized sub-agents
3. Each agent performs its domain task autonomously using AI + external tools
4. Human-in-the-loop checkpoints allow review and approval at each stage
5. Final output: a complete, actionable launch playbook ready for execution

**What it delivers in < 10 minutes:**
- Market & competitor intelligence brief
- 3 buyer personas with messaging angles
- Phased go-to-market strategy
- Ready-to-use launch content (email, social, ads)
- Launch execution tracker with milestones

## 5. Core Features & Capabilities

### Feature 1 — Launch Intelligence Brief
Automated market research report combining competitor analysis, trend signals, and market sizing — generated by the Market Intelligence Agent using real-time web research.

### Feature 2 — Audience Persona Builder
AI-generated buyer personas with demographic profiles, job-to-be-done analysis, pain points, and tailored messaging angles for each segment.

### Feature 3 — Launch Strategy Generator
A phased, milestone-driven go-to-market plan — pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch — with specific actions, channels, and success metrics.

### Feature 4 — Competitive Positioning Map
Visual differentiation matrix showing how LaunchIQ (and by extension, the user's product) compares across key buyer decision criteria.

### Feature 5 — Campaign Content Generator
Strategy-aware launch copy for email sequences, LinkedIn posts, Twitter threads, and Google/Meta ad headlines — generated with persona and strategy context loaded.

### Feature 6 — Launch Execution Tracker
Step-by-step checklist connected to the generated strategy — with status tracking, owner assignment, and deadline management.

### Feature 7 — Performance Analytics Dashboard
Real-time launch performance view with AI-powered recommendations for optimization based on what's working and what isn't.

## 13. Modern Agentic AI Architecture (2026)

### Architecture Philosophy

LaunchIQ is designed as a **2026-native agentic AI system** — not a chatbot wrapper, not a prompt chain, but a true multi-agent orchestration platform where autonomous agents collaborate, use tools, maintain persistent memory, and operate within structured human oversight.

**Core principles:**
- **Agents act, not just answer** — each agent executes tasks using tools, not just generates text
- **Memory is persistent** — agents remember context across sessions and across users
- **Tools extend intelligence** — agents connect to the real world via MCP tool servers
- **Humans stay in control** — HITL checkpoints at every major decision point
- **Everything is observable** — every agent step is traced, logged, and auditable

---

### Agent Architecture

#### Orchestrator Agent (Claude Opus 4.6)
- **Role:** Master coordinator, workflow planner, and decision arbitrator
- **Responsibilities:** Decomposes user goal into sub-tasks, dispatches sub-agents, manages state, synthesizes final output
- **Protocol:** A2A (Agent-to-Agent) message passing
- **Memory:** Full workflow state + user context

#### Market Intelligence Agent (Claude Sonnet 4.6)
- **Role:** Autonomous market researcher
- **Capabilities:** Web search, competitor scraping, trend identification, market sizing
- **Tools:** Tavily Search API, HTML scraper, news aggregator
- **Output:** Structured competitive brief + trend signals

#### Audience Insight Agent (Claude Sonnet 4.6)
- **Role:** Buyer persona specialist
- **Capabilities:** Persona synthesis, segment mapping, messaging angle generation
- **Tools:** Market research databases, persona templates, JTBD framework
- **Output:** 3 buyer personas with messaging recommendations

#### Launch Strategy Agent (Claude Opus 4.6)
- **Role:** GTM strategist
- **Capabilities:** Phased planning, channel selection, milestone generation
- **Tools:** Strategy frameworks, market data, historical launch patterns
- **Output:** Full phased launch plan with milestones and KPIs

#### Content Generation Agent (Claude Sonnet 4.6)
- **Role:** Launch copywriter
- **Capabilities:** Multi-format content generation with persona and strategy context loaded
- **Tools:** Brand voice analyzer, tone checker, A/B variant generator
- **Output:** Email sequences, social copy, ad headlines per persona

#### Analytics & Feedback Agent (Claude Haiku 4.5)
- **Role:** Performance monitor and optimizer
- **Capabilities:** Metric tracking, anomaly detection, optimization recommendations
- **Tools:** GA4 integration, HubSpot data, custom event tracking
- **Output:** Real-time recommendations and performance summaries

### Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Design

HITL is a first-class workflow component in LaunchIQ — not an afterthought.

| Checkpoint | Agent Output | User Action |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Post-research | Competitive brief | Review, edit, approve |
| Post-personas | Audience cards | Edit persona details, approve |
| Post-strategy | GTM plan | Adjust timeline, approve |
| Post-content | Launch copy | Edit copy, approve |
| Post-analytics | Optimization recs | Accept/reject recommendations |

**Autonomy dial:** Users can adjust the HITL frequency — from "approve every step" to "notify me only on major decisions" as trust in the system grows.

---

### Guardrails & Safety Layer

- **Output validation:** All agent outputs validated against structured schemas before rendering
- **Hallucination detection:** Source citations required for all market claims
- **Content filtering:** Brand safety check on all generated copy
- **Rate limiting:** Per-user agent run limits to prevent abuse
- **PII protection:** No personally identifiable information processed or stored without consent
- **Audit logging:** Full immutable log of all agent actions for compliance
