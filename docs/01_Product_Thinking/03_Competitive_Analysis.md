## 3. Competitor Deep Dives

### HubSpot Marketing Hub
- **Strengths:** Massive integration ecosystem, brand trust, all-in-one suite
- **Weaknesses:** Not AI-native, no autonomous agents, expensive, complex for startups
- **Threat Level:** Low (different buyer profile and complexity)

### Jasper AI
- **Strengths:** Strong brand in AI content, easy to use, large user base
- **Weaknesses:** Generic content with no market context, no strategy layer, no agents
- **Threat Level:** Medium (overlaps on content generation)

### Crayon
- **Strengths:** Deep competitive intelligence, enterprise-grade CI workflows
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, CI-only (no launch strategy or content), no agents
- **Threat Level:** Low (CI is one component of LaunchIQ, not full scope)

### Copy.ai GTM
- **Strengths:** GTM-focused content workflows, growing fast
- **Weaknesses:** No autonomous agents, no market research, content-first not strategy-first
- **Threat Level:** Medium-High (closest positioning overlap)

### Notion AI
- **Strengths:** Ubiquitous, flexible, low price point
- **Weaknesses:** General-purpose, no launch intelligence, no agents, no market data
- **Threat Level:** Low (tool, not platform)
