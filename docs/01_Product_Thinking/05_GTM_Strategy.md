## 1. Target Segment

**Primary:** Startup founders and early-stage product marketers at B2B SaaS companies (1–50 employees)

**Secondary:** Growth marketers and marketing managers at scale-up SaaS companies (50–500 employees)

**Buyer Profile:**
- Role: Founder, Head of Marketing, Product Marketer, Growth Lead
- Company: B2B SaaS, early to growth stage
- Pain: No time for manual launch research, need speed and strategy quality
- Budget: $50–$500/month for marketing tools
- Buying behavior: Self-serve, tries before buying, influenced by peers

---

## 2. Positioning Statement

> For startup founders and product marketers who launch B2B SaaS products, **LaunchIQ** is the AI-native launch intelligence platform that **autonomously researches your market, generates your strategy, and creates your content** — so you can launch with confidence in hours, not weeks. Unlike generic AI writing tools or manual research workflows, LaunchIQ uses specialized AI agents that work together to deliver a complete, context-aware launch playbook tailored to your product.

## 5. Launch Phases

### Pre-Launch (Weeks 1–8)
**Goal:** Build audience, validate demand, collect waitlist

- [ ] Set up landing page with waitlist signup
- [ ] Post weekly build updates on LinkedIn + Twitter
- [ ] Publish "How I'm building LaunchIQ with AI agents" content series
- [ ] Reach out to 20 target users for pilot feedback
- [ ] Submit to 5 AI tools directories (Futurepedia, There's An AI For That, etc.)
- [ ] Collect 100+ waitlist emails before launch

**Key Metric:** 100 waitlist signups

---

### Launch Day (Week 9–10)
**Goal:** Maximum visibility spike, first paying users

- [ ] Product Hunt submission (Tuesday–Wednesday for max votes)
- [ ] Show HN post on Hacker News
- [ ] Email waitlist with early access + special pricing
- [ ] LinkedIn post — founder story + product demo video
- [ ] Twitter thread — "I built a 5-agent AI system for product launches, here's how"
- [ ] Personal outreach to 50 warm connections

**Key Metric:** 500 signups in first week, 20 free trial activations

---

### Post-Launch (Weeks 11–16)
**Goal:** Activation, retention, first revenue

- [ ] Onboard pilot users, collect NPS and testimonials
- [ ] Convert free trials to paid (target: 10 paying customers)
- [ ] Iterate on top 3 pain points from user feedback
- [ ] Publish case study from first paying customer
- [ ] Start SEO content plan (target: 10 high-intent keywords)
- [ ] Explore HubSpot App Marketplace listing

**Key Metric:** 10 paying customers, $500 MRR

## 8. Early Traction Tactics

1. **Founder-led content:** Document the build publicly — authenticity converts
2. **Free audits:** Offer to run LaunchIQ on 5 pilot companies' products for free in exchange for testimonials
3. **Community presence:** Active in Marketing Slack communities (Demand Curve, Exit Five)
4. **Build in public:** Weekly metrics tweet (subscribers, MRR, agent run count)
5. **Strategic partnerships:** Co-market with AI-native tools (Notion, Clay, Apollo)
